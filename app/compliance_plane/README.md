# Compliance Plane

The Compliance Plane provides an auditability and attestation governance layer.
It manages canonical requirements, control objectives, evidence traceability,
attestations, exceptions, and compliance debt.

## Key Principles
- **No Evidence-less Compliance**: A policy alone is not enough; evidence is required.
- **Traceability**: Requirements map to controls, which map to evidence.
- **Attestation & Freshness**: Evidence and attestations must be fresh and not expired.
- **Exceptions are not Compliant**: Exceptions carry debt and residual risk.

## Components
- Registry: Canonical source for requirements and controls.
- Mappings: Links requirements to controls and evidence.
- Evidence & Retention: Defines what evidence is needed and how long it's kept.
- Attestations & Certifications: Formal reviews and sign-offs.
- Exceptions & Debt: Manages accepted risks and overdue remediation.
