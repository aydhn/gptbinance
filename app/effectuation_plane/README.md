# Effectuation Plane

The Effectuation Plane provides decision-realization governance.
It answers the question: "Exactly what was implemented, by whom, with what proof, and what is the residual tail?"
It ensures that 'issued' != 'completed' != 'verified effective'.

## Key Concepts
- **Executable Orders**: Orders must be actionable and typed.
- **Dependencies & Deadlines**: Explicit gates and time bounds.
- **Completion Proofs & Verification**: Evidence of actual effect, not just status updates.
- **Sham Compliance & Hidden Rollbacks**: Explicitly tracked and prohibited.
- **Trust Verdicts**: A mandatory gate for finality, liability releases, etc.
