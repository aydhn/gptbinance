# Research Plane & Evidence Funnel Governance

## Why a Research Plane?
In trading systems, chaos often begins in research before it manifests in strategy. Similar ideas are tested repeatedly under different names, failed research is lost, contradictory evidence is ignored, and hypotheses are conflated with compelling narratives. This leads to strategies being promoted on weak foundations.

The Research Plane provides a formal governance layer enforcing a strict, auditable lifecycle:
`Question -> Evidence -> Contradiction -> Confidence -> Readiness -> Thesis Maturation`

## Core Principles
* **Interesting != Experiment-Ready**: A compelling narrative must pass rigorous evidence, falsifiability, and contradiction checks to become experiment-ready.
* **No Silent Deletion**: Failed or invalidated research must be preserved to create organizational memory and prevent duplicate dead-ends.
* **Balanced Evidence**: Evidence bundles must strictly separate supportive and contradictory evidence. Ignoring contradictions degrades research trust.

## Boundaries
This phase does **not** generate alpha or act as an idea dashboard. It establishes the typed, evidence-bound, and contradiction-aware framework that governs the journey from observation to strategy candidate.
