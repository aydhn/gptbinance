# Evidence Pack, Contract Checks, and Go/No-Go Policy

## Evidence Pack
A successful qualification requires evidence from all relevant layers (Security, Release, Ops, Observability, etc.). Missing evidence degrades the score or blocks certification.

## Contract Verification
Checks that the boundaries between components (e.g., Risk -> Portfolio) are intact and schema-compliant.

## Go/No-Go
The final output is a Verdict (PASS, CAUTION, FAIL, BLOCKED) and a Go/No-Go recommendation. The decision is contextual to the profile.
