# Policy Drift & Gap Analysis

## Policy Drift

Policy drift occurs when the declared policy constitution states one verdict, but a module behaves differently (e.g., a module ALLOWs an action that the policy kernel blocks). Identifying drift is critical to maintaining enforcement truthfulness.

## Gap Analysis

Gap analysis identifies areas where safety coverage is missing:
- Uncovered failure classes.
- Stale or missing rules.
- Lack of invariants for critical operations.
