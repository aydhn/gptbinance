# Ablation, Sensitivity, and Regime Split Analysis

A single promising metric is not enough to promote a candidate.
We require:
- **Ablation:** What happens when components are disabled individually?
- **Sensitivity:** How robust is the candidate to small parameter sweeps? Avoid unstable optimums.
- **Regime/Time Split:** Does the candidate improve metrics across different market conditions (e.g., trend vs range) and over distinct time periods?
