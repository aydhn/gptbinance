# Position Integrity: Readiness, Activation ve Incident Entegrasyonu

## position_integrity domain
Introduces a new readiness and reliability domain focused entirely on whether the system knows its position truth.

## Divergence incidents
Incidents are raised if there is a mismatch between the runtime state, the shadow state, or the ledger.

## Runtime/ledger/shadow equivalence
Continuous reconciliation ensures that the high-performance runtime state matches the immutable ledger truth.

## Evidence graph/review packs
Position manifests, divergence reports, and trust verdicts become artifacts in the evidence graph.
