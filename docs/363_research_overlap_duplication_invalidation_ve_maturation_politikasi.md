# Phase 71: Research Overlap, Duplication, Invalidation & Maturation Policy

## Duplicated Research
The system detects when similar questions or hypotheses are re-registered, preventing the proliferation of repeated dead-ends.

## Overlap/Cannibalization
If multiple hypotheses address the same mechanism or market inefficiency, the system generates overlap reports to prevent strategy cannibalization.

## Invalidation History
When a hypothesis is disproven (due to evidence, regime mismatch, or impossibility), its state changes to `invalidated`, capturing the explicit reason. This history prevents future cycles of the same flawed logic.

## Maturation vs. Stagnation
Research items track their progression along the confidence ladder. Items that fail to gather new evidence or resolve contradictions are flagged as stagnant. Dead-end preservation ensures these findings inform future efforts.
