# Phase 71: Research Integrity, Readiness, Activation & Incident Integration Policy

## Research Integrity Domain
The Readiness Board evaluates a new domain: `research_integrity`. Strategies cannot be promoted or activated if their underlying research is degraded or lacks experimental readiness.

## Experiment-Readiness Evidence
Activation guards check that the corresponding research item meets all criteria for `experiment_ready` or `strategy_candidate` before allowing a strategy to proceed.

## Contradiction/Overlap Incidents
The Incident Command System monitors for sudden spikes in unresolved contradictions or critical research overlap, generating alerts and requiring manual intervention.

## Reliability SLOs
Service Level Objectives now track research integrity, measuring factors like "stale evidence burden ceiling" and "required contradiction coverage cleanliness."
