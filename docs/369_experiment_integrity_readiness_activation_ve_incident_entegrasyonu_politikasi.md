# Experiment Integrity Readiness, Activation, and Incident Integration Policy

## Experiment_Integrity Domain
A readiness domain that blocks advancement if experiment integrity is compromised.

## Activation Candidate Expansion Guards
Expansion requests must pass fairness checks before activation.

## Fairness/Baseline Incidents
If fairness breaks critically or baseline drifts, it triggers an incident.

## Reliability SLOs
SLOs track experiment integrity and the proportion of trusted experiment verdicts.

## Evidence Graph/Review Packs
All aspects of the experiment (arms, allocations, fairness reports) form an evidence pack for reviews.
