# Phase 107 - Semantic Plane Architecture

## Core Philosophy
The Semantic Plane introduces "meaning governance" to the trading platform. It ensures that critical business terms, entities, metrics, labels, and status words have explicit, canonical, and typed definitions that are consistent across all planes.

## Flow of Semantic Truth
- **Terms/Entities/Metrics** are mapped to ->
- **Contexts/Units/Thresholds** which resolve into ->
- **Aliases/Conflicts** which dictate ->
- **Interpretations/Trust Verdicts**.

## Why Same Word != Same Meaning
Local jargon, semantic split-brain, and overloaded terms lead to failures in critical decisions. "Ready" in CI/CD is different from "Ready" in trading allocation. Semantic Plane governs these distinctions.

## No Hidden Ambiguity
Glossary documentation is not enough. Ambiguity, semantic drift, translation loss, and label theater are explicitly captured as conflicts and debt, directly blocking unsafe operations across the architecture.
