# Adversarial Integrity: Readiness, Tradeoff, Epistemic, Autonomy, Federation Entegrasyonu

## Adversarial Integrity Domain
Adversarial trust, sistemin readiness ve reliability uzerinde dogrudan etkilidir. Eger adversarial integrity cokmusse (ornegin yaygin metric gaming veya review evasion), readiness blocked olur.

## Tradeoff, Epistemic, Autonomy ve Federation Integrations
*   **Tradeoff:** Metric gaming ve burden hiding incelenir.
*   **Epistemic:** Certainty theater ve contradiction burial incelenir.
*   **Autonomy:** Approval laundering ve self-check gaming incelenir.
*   **Federation:** Partner evidence abuse incelenir.

## Policy Obligations ve Evidence Graph
High-risk action'lar icin adversarial evidence obligastion'lar olusturulur. Evidence graph, adversarial iliskileri (exploits_surface, evades, poisons vb.) modeller.
