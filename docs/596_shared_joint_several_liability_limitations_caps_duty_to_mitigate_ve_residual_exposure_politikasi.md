# Shared, Joint, and Several Liability Policies

## Shared, Joint, Several
- **Shared:** Liability apportioned among multiple actors.
- **Joint:** Each party is independently liable for the full extent.
- **Several:** Each party is liable only for their specific portion.

## Limitations, Caps, and Residual Exposure
- **Limitations & Caps:** Boundaries on the exposure amount or scope.
- **Duty to Mitigate:** The obligation of the harmed party to minimize losses.
- **Residual Exposure:** The liability that remains after caps or indemnities are applied.

## Why Cap != No Exposure
A liability cap limits financial damages but does not erase the underlying fault, responsibility, or non-financial consequences.
