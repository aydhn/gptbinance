# Liability Integrity and Integration Policies

## Liability Integrity Domain
Readiness boards evaluate the clarity of causation, rigor of allocation, and visibility of exposure.

## Cross-Plane Integrations
- **Authority:** Unauthorized actions explicitly generate liability.
- **Remedy:** Remedies reduce damages but do not erase liability records.
- **Contracts:** Breaches must map to specific liability and indemnity structures.
- **Compliance:** Regulatory breaches map to institutional liability.

## Blocker/Caution Semantics
Unresolved liability, incorrect allocations, or hidden exposure act as blockers or cautions for system progression, such as deployments or migrations.
