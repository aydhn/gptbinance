# Restructuring Plans, Liquidations, and Post-Confirmation Policy

## Plans and Classes
Plans cluster claims into classes. Classes are individually designated as "impaired" or "unimpaired". Silent impairment is considered a constitutional violation.

## Confirmation
A plan is a proposal until it is explicitly confirmed. Treating a supported plan as a confirmed plan produces a "plan theater" warning.

## Cures, Haircuts, and Commitments
- **Cures**: Required for assumed commitments.
- **Haircuts**: Visible reductions in distribution.
- **Assumption/Rejection**: Commitments cannot be partially assumed unless formally rewritten.

## Liquidation vs Going Concern
If a plan fails, liquidation tracks actual distributions against remaining deficits. Calling a partial realization "going concern" without sufficient backing produces a misleading comfort warning.

## Post-Confirmation
Confirming a plan does not erase residual duties. Post-confirmation reporting and residual funding duties are explicitly tracked to prevent default.
