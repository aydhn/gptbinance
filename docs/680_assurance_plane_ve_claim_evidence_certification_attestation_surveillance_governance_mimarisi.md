# Assurance Plane Governance Architecture

## Concept
The Assurance Plane transforms generic assertions of "safe/ready/verified" into canonical, evidence-backed objects. It establishes a rigorous pipeline:
Claims + Evidence -> Sufficiency -> Certification -> Attestation -> Surveillance -> Expiry -> Trust.

## Key Principles
* **Documented != Sufficient**: Just because evidence is recorded does not mean it is sufficient for the assurance scope.
* **Sufficient != Certified**: Sufficiency must be explicitly formalized into a certification to be binding.
* **Certified != Attested**: Certifications must be independently or internally attested to confirm non-conflict.
* **Attested != Currently Valid**: Validity demands active, non-lapsed surveillance.

No "false assurance", "stale certification", or "surveillance theater".
