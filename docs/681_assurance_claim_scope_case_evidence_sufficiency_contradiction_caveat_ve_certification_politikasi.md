# Assurance Claims, Scope, Evidence, Sufficiency & Certification

- **Claims & Scope**: An assurance claim is limited by its defined scope. Extrapolating beyond scope is "scope laundering".
- **Evidence & Sufficiency**: Evidence must be strictly sufficient. Partial proof yields a marginal or insufficient verdict.
- **Contradictions & Caveats**: Any contradiction or caveat must be explicitly stated. Suppression degrades trust.
- **Certifications**: Formalize the sufficiency. They explicitly bind the proof to the claim.
