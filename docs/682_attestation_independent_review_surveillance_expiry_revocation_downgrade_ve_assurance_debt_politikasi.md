# Attestation, Surveillance, Expiry, Revocation, Downgrades & Assurance Debt

- **Attestations**: Can be internal, self, or independent.
- **Independent Review**: Guards against self-certification biases.
- **Surveillance & Expiry**: Ongoing checks are necessary. Without surveillance, a certificate is stale or assumed expired.
- **Revocation & Downgrades**: If conditions fail or contradictions emerge, downgrades and revocations trigger automatically.
- **Assurance Debt**: Accumulated when warnings like stale evidence or caveat suppression are ignored.
